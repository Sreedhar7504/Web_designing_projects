Interlaken, Switzerland

A narrow stretch of valley, between the emerald-colored waters

Why Visit Interlaken?

Interlaken for its breathtaking Alpine scenery and adventure sports, including paragliding and hiking, as well as lakeside activities and access to the Jungfrau region, which offers panoramic views from the Jungfraujoch

Top Highlights

Eiger Trail

A famous, mostly downhill hike in the Swiss Alps, near Interlaken, leading from Eigergletscher to Alpiglen

Snow Shoeing

An iconic landmark you can climb for breathtaking views.

White Water Rafting

An exhilarating experience with Class III-IV rapids flowing from the mountains into Lake Brienz.
