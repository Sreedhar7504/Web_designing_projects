Cape Town, South Africa

A modern, cosmopolitan city surrounded by nature.

Why Visit Cape Town?

Cape Town for its diverse natural landscapes, including iconic Table Mountain and pristine beaches like Boulders Beach, as well as its rich history and culture, exemplified by Robben Island and the Bo-Kaap district.

  • • Table Mountain
  • • Victoria & Alfred Waterfront (V&A Waterfront)
  • • Boulders Beach
  • • Robben Island Museum
  • • Cape Point Nature Reserve

Top Highlights

Victoria & Alfred Waterfront (V&A Waterfront)

It's a lively hub for locals and tourists, offering great food, shopping, and access to attractions like the Two Oceans Aquarium.

Robben Island Museum

Offers a poignant glimpse into South Africa's past and the struggle against apartheid.

Cape Point Nature Reserve/h5>

Explore diverse flora and fauna, dramatic cliffs, and incredible ocean vistas.
