London, England

A vibrant, cosmopolitan city with a rich history and a thriving modern culture

Why Visit London?

London for its world-class culture, including free museums like the British Museum, iconic historical landmarks such as Buckingham Palace and Tower of London, vibrant shopping districts, a diverse culinary scene, renowned theatre in the West End, unique neighborhoods, and beautiful Royal Parks.

Buckingham Palace

Tower of London

As the most secure castle in the land, the Tower guarded royal possessions and even the royal family in times of war and rebellion
